Stock Price Predicition

I implemented a stock price prediction model using Gated Recurrent Units (GRU) and Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) networks, leveraging their ability to capture long-term dependencies in sequential data. The model was trained on historical stock price data, incorporating features like opening price, closing price, high, low, and trading volume. By fine-tuning the GRU and LSTM layers, the system learned complex market patterns to generate accurate future price predictions. Performance evaluation was conducted using metrics such as Mean Absolute Error (MAE), Mean Squared Error (MSE), and Root Mean Squared Error (RMSE), ensuring reliable forecasting accuracy.

Tech Stack - Python, LSTM, GRU
